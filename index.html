Getting Around

Atlanta is a city best explored with a mix of walking, rideshares, and a little wandering.

❤ Restaurants · 📍 Activities & Hotel
Getting There
Hartsfield-Jackson Airport
The world's busiest airport, ~25 min from Midtown. Take MARTA rail or a rideshare directly to the hotel.
Around the City
Rideshare & MARTA
Uber/Lyft are abundant. MARTA rail connects the airport, Midtown, and downtown efficiently.
BeltLine
Walk the Trail
The Eastside Trail connects Inman Park to Midtown on foot. Rent e-bikes along the way.
Neighborhoods
Key Areas
Midtown, Old Fourth Ward, West Midtown, Inman Park, Little Five Points — all within 15 min of each other.
