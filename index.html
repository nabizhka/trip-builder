Things To Do

Adventures, culture, and quiet moments — Atlanta has something magical at every turn.

🐋
Georgia Aquarium
The largest aquarium in the world, home to whale sharks, beluga whales, and thousands of marine species. A jaw-dropping experience together.
Must-do
🌿
Atlanta BeltLine
A 22-mile urban trail connecting neighborhoods. Stroll the Eastside Trail lined with murals, cafes, and gardens. Perfect for a slow, romantic afternoon.
Outdoor
🏛️
High Museum of Art
Atlanta's premier art museum with world-class rotating exhibitions and a stunning Renzo Piano-designed building in Midtown.
Culture
🌸
Atlanta Botanical Garden
30 acres of stunning gardens with a canopy walk. Especially magical during spring blooms — a serene and romantic escape.
Romantic
🍹
Rooftop Bar Hopping
Atlanta has some of the South's best rooftop bars. Catch golden hour views of the skyline with cocktails in hand.
Evening
🛍️
Little Five Points & Inman Park
Eclectic boutiques, vintage shops, and beautiful Victorian homes. A charming neighborhood for a lazy morning wander.

Getting There
Hartsfield-Jackson Airport
The world's busiest airport, ~25 min from Midtown. Take MARTA rail or a rideshare directly to the hotel.

Rideshare & MARTA
Uber/Lyft are abundant. MARTA rail connects the airport, Midtown, and downtown efficiently.
BeltLine
Walk the Trail
The Eastside Trail connects Inman Park to Midtown on foot. Rent e-bikes along the way.
